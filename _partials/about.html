PhD Candidate in Computer Science, University of Illinois Chicago | mnipu2@uic.edu

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at University of Illinois Chicago, with a research focus on data visualization, visual computing, human-computer interaction, machine learning, and human-centered AI. I currently work as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Electronic Visualization Laboratory, under the supervision of Dr. G. Elisabeta (Liz) Marai. My work combines computer science, design, and domain expertise to develop interactive systems for exploring complex biomedical, environmental, and urban datasets. I am passionate about turning raw data into actionable insights through interactive visual analytics frameworks, scalable tools, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
